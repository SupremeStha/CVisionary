OUR STORY

Revolutionizing Recruitment with AI

At CVisionary, we're on a mission to transform how job seekers and employers connect by leveraging the power of artificial intelligence to create perfect matches.

Who We Are

CVisionary was founded in 2020 by a team of HR professionals and AI engineers who recognized the inefficiencies in traditional recruitment processes. We saw firsthand how both candidates and employers struggled to find their perfect match, despite the wealth of talent and opportunities available.

Our platform uses cutting-edge AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze resumes, job descriptions, and company cultures to create meaningful connections between job seekers and employers. By focusing on skills, experience, and cultural fit, we help companies build diverse and talented teams while helping individuals find careers where they can truly thrive.

Today, CVisionary serves thousands of companies and job seekers worldwide, with offices in San Francisco, New York, London, and Singapore. Our diverse team of over 120 professionals is united by a common purpose: to make recruitment more efficient, equitable, and human-centered.
